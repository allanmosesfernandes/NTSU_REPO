A huge welcome to all our new students and welcome back to all returners. We hope you had a great summer and are ready to make the most of this year at uni.

To get you started, here’s are some highlights to get involved in this week:
 

Get Into Teaching Donut Van

  Tuesday 27th September

  Clifton Campus

  10am-4pm

Come and grab a tasty donut while learning about how you could become a teacher after finishing university.

 

Headlock Vintage Fair

  Tuesday 27th September

  The Level, City SU,

  10am-5pm

Want to find affordable clothing for your Autumn wardrobe? There’s no better place than our vintage clothing fair. Not only will you find authentic bargains, you’ll also be doing your part for sustainability. That’s a win-win if you ask us.

Brunch & Bevs

  Wednesday 28th September

  The Point, Clifton Campus

  11am-3pm

Join our new Boozy Brunch Club on Wednesdays at The Point. The menu includes a choice of three delicious brunches and either a four-pint pitcher, four VKs, or a bottle of prosecco.

Nasty Gal Sample Sale

  Thursday 29th September

  The Level, City SU,

  10am-4pm

Sample sales, with their reduced prices, are the perfect place to treat yourself to unique pieces from brands you love. Come down to the Nasty Gal sample sale for some great student deals.

Give it A Go-Bowling

  Thursday 29th September

  Nottingham Bowl

  7pm

Want to meet new people, make fun memories, and enjoy some friendly competition? Join us for our first bowling session of the year. You get two games of bowling included in the ticket.

Fluid

  Friday 30th September

  Brack Bar, Brackenhurst Campus

  9:30pm-2am

Brack’s biggest Friday night. Celebrate the start of the weekend with discounted drinks and live DJ’s playing your favourite tunes. Free before 11:30pm / £3 after.
